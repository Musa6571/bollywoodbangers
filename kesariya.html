Kesariya

Hindi Lyrics (in English letters)

Kesariya tera ishq hai piya
Rang jaaun jo main haath lagaun
Din beete saara teri fikar mein
Raataan teri khair manaun

English Translation

Your love is saffron-colored, my beloved
I get colored just by your touch
My days pass worrying about you
My nights are spent praying for your well-being
